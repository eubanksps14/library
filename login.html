Welcome

The Mountain Vista library is now 15 years old! It has been here since the school’s opening in 2001. It’s main reason is to checkout/in books, but recently our library has gone somewhat online! It has always been a place for people to work on homework or projects. Also, people can just hangout in the library on the couches with their friends. The MVHS library is the most social and well utilized part of the school. Along with just being a place to hangout and read, the library offers clubs if you are really into reading. It is referred to as the MVHS Book Club. The MVHS Book Club provides students with the opportunity to meet on a regular basis to discuss books they have read. They meet the second Thursday of every month during SOAR at 8:15 a.m. in the library conference room. The teacher sponsors of this club are Kim Cox and Julie Jacobs. Julie Jacobs is one of Mountain Vista’s media specialists. She is in the library everyday from 7:15 a.m. to 3 p.m. everyday. Along with being a co-sponsor for the book club Kim Cox teaches many design classes. She teaches Interior Design courses I and II. When she is not teaching design classes she is helping with child development and professional internship. Overall the Mountain Vista Library is a very innovative place where there are opportunities to be involved with things such as our book club, but it is also a place to collaborate and be social with your peers.
